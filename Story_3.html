Rademenesa

The healing cat or Rademenesa is a nurse! His activities can surprise you because there is a long history of love and care attached to them. Diagnosed with inflamed resporatory tract at the age of 2 months, Rademenesa lived in an animal sanctuary where he overcame the ordeal with treatment and care. He now lives in an animal shelter where he cares for other sick animals and tries to nurse them back to health. It is quite surprising but you can actually see the cat sitting by an ailing cat or a dog; trying to let it know that there is someone who cares!

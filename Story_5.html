Jack the dog

A German shepherd Jack is an inspiration for all cancer patients. He was diagnosed with cancer when he was 14 months old. He underwent a surgery for six hours in order to remove the tumour that had metastasized all over the ear. The cancer had spread to ear canal and thus the left ear of the dog had to be amputated. The dog amazingly travelled over 5000 miles for getting his cancer treatments including radiation therapy. Eight weeks after diagnosis he was officially cancer free and continues to inspire others to fight for their cause.

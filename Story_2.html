Bob and James Bowen

A homeless musician James Bowen came across a cat in the subway that was suffering from a wound in the leg. It possibly came from a cat fight but the stray cat needed help. James took him to the vet and helped it recover. From them onwards, the two became inseparable. Bob the cat followed James everywhere and revenues dramatically increased for James. Eventually he wrote a book about how Bob changed his life and about their adventures on the street together. If you want to feel how Bob really changed his life- buy the book “The Street Cat named Bob”!
